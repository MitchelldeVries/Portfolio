Overview

Bol.com opened its doors on March 30, 1999. More than twenty years later, the store has 10.5 million active customers in the Netherlands and Belgium and a range of more than 22 million items. Bol.com is one of the most popular stores in the Netherlands and Belgium.

At bol.com, customers find the largest selection in a large number of store categories with books & ebooks in Dutch and other languages, music, films, games, electronics, toys, baby items, garden and DIY items and everything for living, animals, sports, leisure and personal care.

In my case, I joined the team that was working on the order intake process. We always invested time into learning the newest technologies available and building great software in an agile way!
